About Memoto

Memoto offers you the opportunity to join a fast-growing and globally recognized team in a groundbreaking industry. You will be at the forefront of wearable technology and digital services, with the mission to design products that shape how we remember our lives. To find out more about Memoto’s products, visit memoto.com.

After an insanely successful Kickstarter campaign last fall, Memoto is currently preparing the first shipments to go out to our thousands of pre-order customers. This fall, we will take the first steps towards getting Memoto from a pre-launch startup to a global consumer brand.

About the role

As a designer at Memoto you will be defining and designing the Memoto experience closely with our Art Director and UX Lead in the Stockholm office. You will create products and services for web, mobile and print and be a cornerstone in our creative process.

Your designs should both impress and surprise. You show great understanding of user needs and can boil them down to crisp concepts and sketches. You take ownership of projects and lead them from start to finish in collaboration with the rest of the team. You take responsibility for delivery of your projects and allocate your time accordingly, depending on what needs to be accomplished.

We will fill the role as soon as possible.

Skills

- You design to solve problems
- You are familiar with the latest design toolsets and trends
- You are a team player and passionate about your craft
- You are familiar and comfortable with designing for various platforms
(i.e. web, mobile, tablet and print)
- You demonstrate strong communication skills in directing and leading your ideas to greatness
- You love designing for the digital world but also have some experience from packaging and print

You will be working with

- Designing app interfaces (iOS, Android, OSX, Windows etc.)
- Design of E-commerce/web interfaces
- User testing
- Creating brochures, folders and other marketing material, as needed
- Content creation for blog and social media
- Marketing campaigns
